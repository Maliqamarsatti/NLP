Coffee leaf rust is caused by Hemileia vastatrix. It shows yellow-orange spots. Treat with copper-based fungicide and prune infected leaves.
Phoma appears as dark brown-black spots. Prune and use appropriate fungicide.
Cercospora causes small brown-gray lesions. Improve drainage and spray fungicide.
Leaf miner causes winding trails in leaves. Remove affected leaves and use neem oil or insecticide.
